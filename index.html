About Cromch

Cromch is a tool for embracing intentionally "bad" aesthetics or to quickly optimize (and crop) an image.


A, B, C, D options represent JPEG, WebP, GIF and image scaling compression artifacts. Output is in JPG and fixed at 1000px width.


All processing is local.

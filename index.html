About Cromch

Cromch is a local, client‑side image editor for embracing intentionally "bad" aesthetics. Load an image and tweak:

  • JPEG quality, WEBP/GIF artifacts, and scaling
  • Adjustments like saturation, contrast, brightness
  • Optional motion blur and noise
  • Crop and add text layers

All processing happens in your browser—no uploads.
